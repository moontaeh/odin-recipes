Bolognese

Description

Easy to make bolognese to feed your family. Make a bunch and save them as leftovers as they heat very easily

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Prego
  • 1 large Onion
  • 1 pack of mushrooms
  • 1lb ground beef

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta alongside the rest of the steps
  2. Dice the onions and mushrooms
  3. Saute the onion and mushrooms until the onions are translucent
  4. Put in the ground beef and cook until browned
  5. Put in the prego and mix it all together
  6. Plate the pasta then the bolognese sauce on top and serve.
