Pasta Salad

Description

A refreshing pasta salad to liven up the week. Create a large amount and portion it out through the week to save money from those pesky lunch meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Pasta
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 cucumber
  • 200g cherry tomatoes
  • 1lb chicken breast

Steps

  1. Boil some water and put the pasta in until it is al-dente
  2. Chop up all the vegetables into bite sized pieces
  3. Boil or fry the chicken over a pan
  4. Mix all the ingredients together
